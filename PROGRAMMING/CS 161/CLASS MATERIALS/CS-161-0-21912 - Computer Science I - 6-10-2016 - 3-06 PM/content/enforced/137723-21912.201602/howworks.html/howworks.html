The format for this class is fairly simple and straight-forward. We will cover the first 10 chapters of the text at a pace of one chapter per week. The only exceptions to this are that we will not cover a chapter during the week of the midterm and we will cover two chapters in the first week.

Each week there will be the ppt files for the chapter along with notes that I have created to go along with the slides. As a student you will be responsible for reading through the chapter, viewing the slides, and reading my notes.

Most weeks will also have a programming assignment. There are a total of 9 programming assignments for this class. Be sure to pay attention to the due date for the assignments.

One of the most valuable resources available to you as a distance learner is the discussion board. You should read this frequently and post any time you have questions.

I will be monitoring the discussion board and checking email regularly so that I can respond if there are any questions.
